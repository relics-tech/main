About Us

At Relics, we understand that finding reliable and quality tech support and PC building assistance can often be expensive and frustrating. That's why we've made it our mission to provide these services completely free of charge.

Our team of dedicated volunteers is passionate about technology and committed to helping you resolve any technical issues you may encounter. Whether you're building your first PC or troubleshooting complex system problems, we're here to guide you every step of the way. We pride ourselves on offering personalized, one-on-one support to ensure you get the best possible experience and solutions tailored to your specific needs.

What We Offer

Free PC Building

Get expert advice on selecting components and building your dream PC without spending a dime. We'll help you maximize performance within your budget.

Free Tech Support

Troubleshoot hardware and software issues with our knowledgeable team. From boot problems to performance optimization, we've got you covered.

Custom AI Assistant

Our unique AI can provide instant answers to common tech questions and guide you through basic troubleshooting steps anytime, day or night.

Our Staff

Our Staff Program

At Relics Tech Support, we're always looking for passionate individuals to join our volunteer team. There are no quotas or mandatory hours - you help when you have time and energy to spare.

Whether you're a tech enthusiast looking to share your knowledge or a professional wanting to give back to the community, we welcome you to apply. Our flexible system ensures that helping others remains enjoyable and never feels like an obligation.
