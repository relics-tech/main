About Us

At Relics, we understand that finding reliable and quality tech support and PC building assistance can often be expensive and frustrating. That's why we've made it our mission to provide these services completely free of charge.

Our team of dedicated volunteers is passionate about technology and committed to helping you resolve any technical issues you may encounter. Whether you're building your first PC or troubleshooting complex system problems, we're here to guide you every step of the way. We pride ourselves on offering personalized, one-on-one support to ensure you get the best possible experience and solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Rank System

Jr. Specialist

As a Junior Specialist, you demonstrate solid technical knowledge and active participation in our community. To qualify, you need:

  • Consistent activity in the server
  • Helping to promote community growth
  • Maintain 30+ stars from ratings (average 6+/10 across 5 reviews)

Specialist

Specialists have proven their skills as Junior Specialists and consistently provide excellent support. Requirements include:

  • Consistent high-quality assistance
  • Positive feedback from community members
  • Demonstrated reliability and knowledge

Sr. Specialist

Our most dedicated members who go above and beyond. Senior Specialists receive additional permissions and responsibilities.

  • Long-term commitment to the community
  • Exceptional technical knowledge
  • Leadership qualities and mentoring ability

Ticket Guide

Tech Support Tickets

#tech-support channel

Our tech support tickets provide direct access to our trained specialists who can help diagnose and resolve your technical issues. Whether you're dealing with hardware malfunctions, software errors, or system performance problems, we're here to help.

When opening a ticket, you'll be asked to provide details about your issue, what troubleshooting steps you've already tried, and information about your operating system. This helps our specialists quickly understand your situation and provide the most effective assistance.

PC Building Tickets

#pc-building channel

Our PC building service helps you create the perfect custom computer tailored to your specific needs and budget. Whether you're building a gaming powerhouse, a content creation workstation, or an everyday productivity machine, our specialists will guide you through component selection and optimization.

The ticket form will ask about your budget, intended use for the PC, and any specific preferences you have. This information allows us to recommend components that perfectly match your requirements.
